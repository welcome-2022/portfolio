Les Paul

The Gibson Les Paul is a solid body electric guitar that was first sold by the Gibson Guitar Corporation in 1952. The guitar was disigned by factory manager John Huis and his team with input from and endorsement by guitarist Les Paul.

Flying V

Introduced in 1958 and only made in limited quantities, the Gibson Flying V has taken flight over the years along with legions of followers. This Flying V carries heritage to new heights. The Antique natural finish, and slimTaper neck provide classic looks, sound, and playability.

Thunderbird Bass Guitar

The Gibson Thunderbird has the classic reverse body and headstock design as originally introduced in 1963 as Gbson's first neck-through-body bass design. The narrow nut width and rounded neck profile neck feels both fast and intuitive. The thunderbird high output, can deliver the sonic and iconic low end voice for which the Thunderbird is known.
